Support & Resources

Need help connecting to your onboard server or interpreting dashboards? This page covers the essentials and how to get in touch with our team.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect to my boat server?

Install the Compendium Nav server package on your onboard Raspberry Pi or preferred device. Once paired, the mobile app discovers the server on the local network and maintains the link automatically.

What platforms are supported?

Compendium Nav runs on iPhone, iPad, and modern desktop browsers. The onboard relay supports standard marine data sources, including Signal K and Bluetooth sensors.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. Operational data remains on your boat server. When you access the app away from your boat, the VPS relays data securely without persisting it.

Can I customize dashboards?

Yes. The app lets you arrange widgets for tides, anchor watch, weather, and custom instrument panels to match your workflow.
