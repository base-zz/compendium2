Everything you need to stay ahead on the water

Anchor Awareness

Monitor rode, position, AIS targets, and drag trends in real time while receiving alerts when something changes unexpectedly.

Data Fusion

Combine data from disparate systems into one unified view, including NMEA2000, Bluetooth, LoRa, Zigbey, and Z-Wave. Let Compendium Nav surface critical metrics automatically across all your devices.

Dashboards

Design custom dashboards and group data by situation—at anchor, in the marina, or underway—or by systems like electrical, wind, tanks, and navigation.

Remote Connectivity

Whether you are on deck or shoreside, Compendium Nav securely relays data through our private server.

How Compendium Nav works

  • Onboard server: A trusted device (Raspberry Pi or similar) collects Signal K, sensor, and weather feeds.
  • Secure relay: When off-vessel, encrypted traffic routes through our VPS for conveniencenever for storage.
  • Unified app: iOS and web dashboards present information with consistent layouts for quick decisions.
